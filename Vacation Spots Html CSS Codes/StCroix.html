Chenay Bay Beach Resort
A casual and relaxing boutique resort located on picturesque Chenay Bay Beach, on sunny St Croix, America’s Paradise, Set among native foliage and sweeping palms, Chenay Bay Beach Resort is comprised of rooms in cottages, located between indigenous plants and trees.

Chenay Bay Beach Resort is centrally located on the North shore of St Croix, only minutes from downtown historic Christiansted and the charming boardwalk and downtown tourist shopping, and only 15 minutes from the island’s main airport. The seaplane is a convenient 15 minute drive to the quaint harbor in old downtown Christiansted.

The U.S. Virgin Islands are a group of Caribbean islands and islets. A U.S. territory, it's known for white-sand beaches, reefs and verdant hills. St. Thomas island is home to the capital, Charlotte Amalie. To the east is the island of St. John, most of which comprises Virgin Islands National Park. St. Croix island and its historic towns, Christiansted and Frederiksted, are to the south. The United States Virgin Islands enjoy a tropical climate, with little seasonal change throughout the year.
